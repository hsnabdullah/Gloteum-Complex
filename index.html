Our Mission

Earning Yield from Gold Has Never Been So Simple

Golteum is a financial inclusion platform that offers physical gold investing via fractional NFTs thus democratizing the process, enabling all types of investors to have access to the precious asset.

All Golteum users can access industry leading NFT trading tools through a seamless interface on mobile app and desktop browser. Each Gold bar that we mint is backed by the physical version of Gold 99.9999% purity, which is fully insured and stored in one of our secure depositary vaults. The Gold bar is split into fractions making it easy for anyone to invest in the Gold from as little as one gram which could cost around $60.
